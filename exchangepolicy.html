Return & Exchange Policy

1. Review Eligibility

Before initiating a return or exchange, please ensure that your item meets our eligibility criteria. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in their original packaging, complete with all tags attached. Certain items, such as underwear and swimwear, may be non-returnable for hygiene reasons. If you're unsure whether your item qualifies, please consult our customer support for clarification.

2. Contact Customer Support

To begin the return or exchange process, simply reach out to our customer support team. You can contact us via email at trendify@gmail.com or through WhatsApp at 011-1110057. When contacting us, please provide your order number and a brief explanation of the reason for your return or exchange. Our friendly team is here to assist you and will guide you through the next steps.Once your request is received, our customer support team will respond promptly with detailed instructions on how to proceed. If your return is due to a defective or damaged item, you may be provided with a prepaid return shipping label. This will make the return process as convenient as possible for you. Be sure to follow the instructions closely to ensure a smooth return or exchange.

3. Pack & ship the Item

When you’re ready to return the item, please pack it carefully in its original packaging. Ensure that all tags, accessories, and any accompanying documentation are included. Proper packing helps protect the item during transit and ensures that it remains in sellable condition. This step is crucial for processing your return or exchange efficiently.After packing the item, it’s time to ship it back to us. If you received a prepaid shipping label, simply print it out, attach it to your package, and drop it off at the designated shipping location. If you’re covering the shipping costs yourself, please choose a reliable carrier and retain the tracking number for your records. This will help you monitor your return shipment.

4. Track & Exchange Confirmation

Once you’ve shipped the item, keep an eye on the tracking number provided by your carrier. This allows you to monitor the status of your return until it arrives at our warehouse. Tracking your shipment is important for ensuring it reaches us safely and on time.After we receive your returned item, our team will inspect it to confirm that it meets our return policy criteria. If everything checks out, we will process your refund or exchange request. Refunds will be issued back to your original payment method within 7 business days. If you’re exchanging an item, we’ll ship out your new item based on availability.

5. Enjoy Your New Item!

Once your return or exchange is complete, you can enjoy your new item or the refund to your original payment method. If you have any questions or need assistance throughout the process, our customer support team is just a message away. We’re committed to ensuring you have a positive shopping experience!
